# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, specific, and technically honest.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abuse
- Doxxing or sharing sensitive documents
- Bad-faith bug reports or intentionally misleading reproductions

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to keep collaboration useful and safe.
